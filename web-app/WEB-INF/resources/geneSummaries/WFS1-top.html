WFS1 encodes the transmembrane protein wolframin, which regulates calcium levels in the endoplasmic reticulum and plays a role in the ER\'s unfolded protein response. A 2010 study in the Journal of Clinical Investigation found that the gene suppresses ER stress signaling by upregulating a key transcription factor (ATF6α) and downregulating the E3 ubiquitin ligase HRD1. Pancreatic β cells without functioning copies of WFS1 exhibit dysregulated ER stress signaling, eventually leading to cell death.
