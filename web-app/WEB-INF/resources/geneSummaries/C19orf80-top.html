C19orf80, alias ANGPTL8, encodes a hormone called betatrophin (also called lipasin). In 2013, betatrophin was reported to dramatically promote pancreatic β cell proliferation, expand β cell mass, and improve glucose tolerance in mice. Its mechanism of action remains to be elucidated; according to the 2013 paper, the hormone does not appear to contribute to insulin resistance, a known cause of β cell proliferation. Variants in C19orf80 have also been repeatedly linked with several lipid traits in mouse and human studies.
